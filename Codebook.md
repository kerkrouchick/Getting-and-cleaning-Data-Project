## Subject

30 subjects/volunteers who participated in the experiments (numbered 1 - 30)

## Activity

6 activities performed by each subject - walking, walking_upstairs, walking_downstairs, sitting, standing, laying. Originally, this data was provided as numbers from 1-6. Linked data with activity_labels data to match it to the correct activity.

## Average Measurements (columns 3 - 68)

The average value of the means and stds of the 561 measurements provided in the original datasets. See features_info.txt from the original zip file for more info on this data.
